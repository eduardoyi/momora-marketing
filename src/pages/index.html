Here's the thing about real memories

Photos are great. But they can't capture what your kid actually said. Or how it felt when they finally did the thing.

We built Momora because we wanted to save the stories, not just the snapshots. The moments that make you laugh at 2am when you're up with the baby, remembering when your toddler called butterflies "flutter-byes."

Keep it simple: type it, say it, or add the photos and videos already on your phone. Save it as plain text or let Momora turn the story into an illustration. Your family journal keeps it all together.

Easy enough to do one-handed
