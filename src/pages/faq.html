Frequently Asked Questions

How Momora works, what stays private, and what to expect

Memories & AI

Momora is a private family memory journal for the moments that are easy to forget. Save a story as text, dictate it by voice, attach photos or videos, and optionally turn written memories into AI illustrations.

You can save plain text, text with an AI illustration, or a photo/video memory with an optional caption. A media memory can contain 1–10 photos or videos. Images can be up to 20 MB, and videos can be up to 60 seconds and 100 MB.

Tap to record for up to two minutes. Momora transcribes and cleans up the recording, then places the text and suggested family tags in the editor for you to review. The audio is processed temporarily and is not stored after transcription.

No. For a written memory, turn off the AI option to save it as plain text. You can also attach your own photos or videos instead. AI character portraits are used when you choose the illustrated-memory experience.

You add a profile photo for a child or family member, and Momora creates a consistent illustrated character. When you save an illustrated text memory, Momora analyzes its tone and uses the ready portraits of tagged family members as references. AI output can be imperfect, and you can retry a failed or unwanted illustration.

You can tag up to four family profiles in a memory. Momora can suggest tags when a name or nickname appears in your text, and you can change the selection before saving.

The app interface is currently in English. You can enter memory text in other languages, but transcription and AI results may vary by language.

Family Sharing

Yes. An owner or manager can create a private, single-use invite code. The invited person redeems it, and an owner or manager approves the request after checking their name and email.

The owner controls the family journal and is the only person who can delete it. Managers can add, edit, and delete memories and child profiles, invite members, and moderate comments. Viewers can browse the journal and may like or comment, but cannot change journal entries or profiles.

Yes. Every active family role can like and comment. Momora shows aggregate like counts without a public liker list. Comments show the account display name, and their author or a family owner/manager can delete them.

They lose access immediately. Memories and child profiles they added stay with the family journal unless an owner or manager deletes them. If the person's account is later permanently deleted, the remaining content no longer identifies them as its creator.

Account, Privacy & Pricing

Momora is not a public social network. Journal data is limited to active members of the family, with role-based access controls. Photos, videos, portraits, and illustrations are kept in private storage and opened through time-limited signed links. Only invite people you trust.

Depending on the feature, Momora sends relevant memory text, profile details, profile photos or portraits, the first photo in a photo memory, or temporary voice audio to OpenAI. We send only what is needed for the requested feature. OpenAI states that API data is not used to train its models by default. See our Privacy Policy for the complete breakdown.

Images attached to memories are re-encoded before upload to remove EXIF data, including GPS and device metadata. Momora may use a selected photo's capture date on your device to suggest the memory date, but it keeps only the date. Video-container metadata is not currently stripped.

Enter your email address and Momora sends a six-digit, one-time code. The production app does not require a password or a Google or Apple social-login account.

Not in the current version. There are no in-app subscriptions, credit packs, or paywalls. If paid features are added later, pricing and renewal terms will be shown before purchase.

A full journal-export feature is not available in the current version. Keep your own copies of important original photos and videos.

In the app, go to Settings → Delete account. Deletion is scheduled for 15 days later, and you can cancel it from Settings during that grace period. If you cannot access the app, use our account-deletion form.

Family owners: journals you own are hidden as soon as deletion is scheduled and permanently deleted with all their content after 15 days unless you cancel.

Deleting a memory removes its text, tags, media, illustration, likes, and comments. There is no recycle bin or recovery feature, so confirm before deleting.
